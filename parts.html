Wheels

P101

The P101 is made to create the ultimate street-performance wheels by combining the purposeful lightweight design of the race wheels with an unmatched level of slick to ensure the best possible friction during street races. It also uses Aerospace-grade 6061-T6 forged aluminum for high strength-to-weight ratio as well as standard side cuts and backpad pocketing to minimize unsprung mass and rotational inertia for maximum performance and handling.

Made In USA SUV Load Rated in diameters 20"-23"

Suspensions

Novitec Torado

The Novitec Torado KW Aluminium Coil Over Sport Suspension for the Lamborghini Aventador is hydraulically height-adjustable, includes dual adjustable aluminium shock absorbers with separated expansion reservoir at damper housing,compression and rebound adjustable in front and rear. This would allow easier and safer driving at those areas to better enjoy driving the Lamborghini.

Made in USA Lowering FA: 15-35mm Material: Stainless steel

Exhaust

Capristo Sports Exhaust

The Capristo high performance sports exhaust system for the Lamborghini Aventador with remote control exhaust valves is made from superior grade T309 stainless steel and represents one of the finest exhaust systems in the world. The system includes the Capristo remote control kit for valve operation, allowing the driver to select loud or quiet modes at the touch of a button;as well as remote control functionality to lift the rear spoiler.

Made In Germany Material: Stainless Steel

ECU

Cantronic

The Novitec Torado Cantronic ensures the lifting of the Rear Wing on the Lamborghini Aventador, helping engine ventilation until a speed of 110km/h. This prevents an overheating of the engine, loss of power and prevents error indications. This allows the lamborghini to lift the rear door wing without breaking it and also allows drivers to speed up without causing damage from the car, allowing drivers to put their mind at ease when speeding.

Made In USA Control Unit: Below 180km/h
